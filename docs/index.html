Help us tell the untold story of climate change

Climate change is complicated, and can often feel as if it is happening far away. However, itâ€™s part of our everyday lives, and the effects are all around us.

Story:Web will be a digital platform that tells stories around climate change using museum objects, sounds, images and words. Begin your journey with a grey seal skull, see and hear how it connects with coastal landslides and St Cuthbert, then share and create your own part of the story. Discover and share images, sounds, and words that bring the stories to life. Where might the journey take us?

We want to grow Story:Web with you, and we need your help to make more connections.

How can I help grow Story:Web?

  • Follow #GrowStoryWeb on Facebook , Twitter or Instagram .
  • Help us decide where to take the story next by commenting on our posts.
  • Add your own images, sounds and words and tell us where they connect, using the hashtag #GrowStoryWeb.

Explore the current Story:Web here .

Your contributions could be included as part of the Reimagining Museums for Climate Action exhibition at Glasgow Science Centre where we will tell the story of Story:Web as part of the UN Climate Change Conference COP26 .
